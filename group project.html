ENSG Digital Academy Wins Best Tech Institution Award 2025

Published: February 1, 2025

ENSG Digital Academy has been recognized as the Best Tech Institution of 2025 at the annual National Education Excellence Awards held in Abuja. The award was presented in recognition of the academy's outstanding contribution to digital education and youth empowerment across Nigeria.

The academy's rector, who received the award on behalf of the institution, expressed gratitude to students, staff, and industry partners who have continuously supported the academy's vision of building world-class digital professionals. This milestone marks a proud moment in the academy's history.

Students Graduate from Intensive Web Development Bootcamp

Published: February 10, 2025

Over 120 students successfully completed the 12-week intensive Web Development Bootcamp organized by ENSG Digital Academy. The graduation ceremony was held at the academy's main auditorium and was attended by industry professionals, parents, and faculty members.

Graduates were trained in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks such as React and Node.js. Several graduates have already secured internship offers from leading tech companies, a testament to the quality and relevance of the academy's curriculum.

ENSG Digital Academy Partners with Global Tech Giants for Internship Program

Published: February 18, 2025

ENSG Digital Academy has signed a landmark partnership agreement with several global technology companies including Microsoft, Google, and local tech firms to provide internship and job placement opportunities for its graduates. The partnership is expected to benefit over 500 students annually.

This collaboration will also see industry experts delivering guest lectures, mentorship sessions, and real-world project experiences to students currently enrolled in the academy's various programs. It represents a significant step forward in bridging the gap between academic training and industry demands.
